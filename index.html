About Us

JNTUHUCEJ - Department of CSE

Welcome to CreSencE 2k26, the flagship national-level technical symposium hosted by the Department of Computer Science & Engineering at JNTUH University College of Engineering Jagtial.

Established with a vision to foster technical excellence and innovation, our college has always been at the forefront of shaping future engineers. CreSencE reflects this spirit, offering a platform where technology meets creativity.

"Waves of Atlantis" invites you to dive deep into an ocean of opportunities. From intense Hackathons and Coding challenges to mesmerizing Cultural nights, we have curated an experience that celebrates the convergence of intellect and art. Join us in this digitally immersive journey!
